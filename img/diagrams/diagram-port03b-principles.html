Decision Framework

Three Lenses

Choosing a visual direction that would hold up across six case studies — evaluated through signal, pattern, and a tiebreaker, not gut feel.

Signal
What does it say before you read it?
Dark Spare signals senior IC who ships — not a junior showing off Figma tricks. The restraint itself is the message: confident enough to let the work lead.
Pattern
What system does it create?
The dark canvas + single accent color creates a repeatable container system — every case study, diagram, and interaction lives in the same visual language without competing for attention.
Decision
What broke the tie?
Diagrams. Dark Spare was the only direction where technical diagrams, data visualizations, and interactive elements felt native — not bolted on. The case study content is the portfolio's visual identity.
