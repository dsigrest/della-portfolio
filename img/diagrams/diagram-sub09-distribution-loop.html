No entry point — every node blocked by the others

Reddit's content rating system was the gatekeeper — it took days to categorize new subreddits, leaving them invisible to recommendations during the window that determined whether they survived or died.

Breaking this loop required an intermediary signal — something that could give communities a category instantly, bypassing the days-long rating process.
