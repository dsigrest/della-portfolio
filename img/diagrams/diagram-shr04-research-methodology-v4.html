Share-ers (Redditors)
Phase 1
Test Existing Experience
Redditors use current sharing flow. Identify breakdowns, friction points, and workarounds (screenshot behavior).
Phase 2
Test Redesigned Version
Same participants validate new experience. Confirm breakdowns are addressed. Measure comprehension and confidence shift.
Recipients (Non-Redditors)
Parallel
Preview Comprehension Tests
Non-Redditors evaluate different preview formats. What makes sense, what doesn't, and what they'd expect after tapping.
Synthesis
Behavioral Model
Findings converge into a model of sharing motivations and inhibitors — the foundation for every design decision in the project.
