Why it mattered

Moderators saw everything at once with no sense of sequence — horizontal scrolling, external links, and a blank composer offered no guidance on what to do first.

This experience was the baseline the progressive disclosure system replaced — structured actions ordered by lifecycle stage instead of a wall of unsequenced content.
