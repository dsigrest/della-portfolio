Failure States

Graceful Degradation

When synthesis quality dips below threshold, the page doesn't break — it falls back to sourced answers instead

Below Threshold
Synthesis suppressed — quality below confidence threshold
Raw source posts surface as fallback
Above Threshold
AI synthesis visible above fold with citations
Source posts linked as cited references
threshold

Design principle: Degradation is not failure. When synthesis quality drops, suppress the synthesis — don't show low-confidence content. The fallback (threaded sources) is the reliable baseline; synthesis is the enhancement.
