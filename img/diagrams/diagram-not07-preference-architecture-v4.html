Label Shift
Changed from vague frequency words to language that maps directly to algorithm behavior. Off → No updates · Low → Popular posts · Frequent → All posts. The label now explains the outcome, not the setting.
Predictable Inbox
Each option connects to a concrete behavior users can anticipate. Popular posts = filtered signals from the algorithm. All posts = unfiltered. Users can predict what their inbox will look like.
Algorithm Transparency
Naming the algorithm behavior gives users mental models. They understand why they see fewer posts — the system is filtering for quality, not arbitrarily limiting delivery.
