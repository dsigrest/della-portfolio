About

Senior Product Designer — Engagement Systems, Growth, AI

I spent five years at Reddit designing engagement systems — inbox, notifications, push, email, chat — usually as the first designer to touch them. Five consecutive team rotations building vision from scratch, aligning cross-functional leadership, and shipping scalable patterns. I joined as an L2 designer in 2021, promoted to Senior (L4) by 2023, and worked directly with the Head of Design and CPO on product strategy. My work spanned growth experimentation, platform foundations, and AI-forward product design — including Reddit's first LLM-powered product.

I start with the problem, not the solution. The most impactful work I've done wasn't polished interfaces — it was invisible infrastructure: design patterns that let teams ship faster, decision frameworks that prevented misalignment, processes that made complex product questions answerable. I prototype in Figma and code (HTML/CSS/JS) depending on what fidelity the question requires. I'm currently exploring AI in design practice, designing for non-deterministic systems, and the intersection of design and developer tools.
