About

Senior Product Designer — Engagement Systems, Growth, AI

I design systems that drive engagement, growth, and clarity at scale.

Over five years at Reddit, I worked across inbox, notifications, push, email, chat, growth, and AI — often as the first designer in ambiguous spaces. My role was typically to bring structure where none existed: define the vision, align cross-functional teams, and turn complexity into shippable systems.

The most valuable work I've done hasn't always been visible UI. It's been the infrastructure behind execution: patterns that help teams move faster, frameworks that improve decisions, and prototypes that make hard product questions answerable.

I work fluidly between strategy and craft, using Figma or code (HTML/CSS/JS) depending on what the problem needs.

Currently interested in AI-native product experiences, non-deterministic systems, design/developer workflows, and products that shape user behavior at scale.
