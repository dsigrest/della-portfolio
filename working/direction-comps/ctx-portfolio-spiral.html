The governance model

Here's how the collaboration actually worked, session to session:

Prompt → Generate → Review → Iterate. I'd define a task. Claude generated options. I reviewed for accuracy, tone, and strategic fit. If it missed the mark, I redirected with more specificity. If it was strong, it went into the build log and moved to the next task.

Human approval was required before any content went live. The AI proposed. The human disposed.

4 Iterate
